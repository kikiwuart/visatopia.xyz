Welcoming

by Soovin Choi

I made a projection mapping video on the shadow box and color wire object. Welcoming hand with colorful wires represents welcoming city, San Jose. I filmed San Jose downtown, and did projection mapping on the shadow box for the three dimensional effect. I want to express this warmth feeling from this city with hands.
