Kālacakra, or time wheel, has the meaning of mortality, endless life circle of time. The traditional design of Kālacakra is dense and layered, in which every layer symbolizes a ritual and sacred practice of Buddhism, it could be the ancient idea of multiple universes, that everything is happening at the same time. I was inspired by its comprehensive style which advocates the idea of equality for all living beings. Holding a time wheel and spinning it, we can view all the details with flexible viewpoints.
